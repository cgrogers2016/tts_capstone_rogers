Together, we can feed our children.

Our mission is to ensure that all children in Chapel Hill-Carrboro have access to nutritious food during the summer. In 2018, 27% - or more than 3,000 children - are food insecure in Chapel Hill-Carrboro City Schools. Food for the Summer works with the entire community to ensure these children are fed.
